[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows falling on the hardwood floor
Leave the rush outside and lock the door
Your eyes are pulling me completely in
I want to memorize the rhythm of your skin

[Verse 1]
A summer breeze is blowing through the blinds
We left the heavy weight of day behind
Just you and me beneath this amber fire
A quiet room that echoes my desire

[Pre-Chorus]
I feel the space between us melting down
We are the only beating hearts in town
I want to trace the edges of your grace
And get completely lost inside this space

[Chorus]
Behind closed doors, it is only you and me
A hidden flame for no one else to see
I lose my breath inside your midnight eyes
We watch the morning waiting in the skies

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, everything we are is ours to keep
Falling in your current, running deep

[Verse 2]

[Bridge]

We are riding on a feeling, burning slow
The tension pulling at the very seams

Saeed is falling deep into the tide

[Spoken]
